Our Mission

Our goal is to help users find their MyChart login pages quickly and without confusion. We provide a single point of reference that simplifies your navigation through the many patient portals available from various providers.

This website was built to serve patients and caregivers who are looking for easy access to their MyChart systems. Whether you're managing your own care or supporting someone else, our pages are structured to guide you through the process of finding the right portal link.

We do not offer any medical services, advice, or record-keeping. We exist only to streamline the process of locating MyChart-related resources already made available by healthcare organizations.

Our team carefully selects and lists portal links that are commonly searched by users. We strive to keep this information current and helpful, without requiring signups or collecting user data.

We believe in online privacy and never request or store any sensitive information. Every visit to our site is anonymous and ad-free in its purest form. The platform exists purely for navigational value.

As digital health access grows, having a trusted place to locate your portal link saves time and reduces frustration. We aim to continue expanding and improving our index to better serve those needs.

If you have questions about your account, need password assistance, or want support, please reach out directly to your healthcare provider through the linked portal site.
